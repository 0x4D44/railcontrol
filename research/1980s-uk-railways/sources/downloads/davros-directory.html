Liverpool Street

The old Liverpool Street station used entirely mechanical signalling, with 5 men in two signal boxes controlling over 400 points and signals. There is a complete diagram (170k), a version reduced at 3:1 (20k), and a small extract at full size (11k) to give you a feel.
